Rent Stadium

Description

In this time most of the young people they got interest about playing sports but the problem is they can’t find a decent places to play their favorite sports , Also we find another problem about searching for small stadium , So we find a solution that we make an application to collect all the information about small stadium such as the location , the price and if the stadium is available or not. The application will be collect all the data about the stadiums and the user will search for it by adding his address or any location and showing him a mini map and a picture for the stadium he wants to rent and he can buy it by cash or using credit card through the application. Also if the users has any problem or any issue they can contact with the support team by reporting them throw the E-mail .
